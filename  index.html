💡 General Tips for Electrical Students

  1. 🔴 Prioritize High-priority skills first — they are your foundation and directly impact placements/GATE.
  2. 🟡 Medium priority — important but can be done alongside high-priority ones.
  3. 🟢 Low priority — nice to have, do them if you have extra time or specific interest.
  4. Consistency beats intensity — 1 hour daily for 60 days is far better than 10 hours in one week.
  5. Build a portfolio — Maintain a GitHub repository + a simple blog documenting your projects.
  6. Use NPTEL heavily — It's free, taught by IIT professors, and respected in Indian academia and industry.
  7. Participate in competitions — e-Yantra (IIT Bombay), Smart India Hackathon, TI Innovation Challenge.
  8. Buy components smartly — Robu.in, Amazon, Element14, and JLCPCB (for PCBs) are great for hardware.
